Easy Avocado Toast

A quick, healthy breakfast ready in 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 lemon
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional toppings:
    • Red pepper flakes
    • Everything bagel seasoning
    • Fried egg

Instructions

  1. Toast bread to your liking
  2. Scoop avocado flesh into a bowl and mash with fork
  3. Mix in a squeeze of lemon juice and salt
  4. Spread avocado mixture on toasted bread
  5. Add optional toppings if desired
  6. Serve immediately
